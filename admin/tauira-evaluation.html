Quality 1 - Tamatekapua

Successful Māori students understand core Māori values

Characteristic

An ability to model the most meaningful qulities in Māori culture, portrayed by way of aroha (love), manaaki (care) and wairua (spritually)

Application to school and work
  1. Manaakitangan - ability to care and be hospitable to others
  2. Kotahitanga - ability to commit to a kaupapa/vision
  3. Wairuatanga - moral compass and sense of social justice
